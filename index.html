About Me

I am a fourth-year undergraduate at Hunter College CUNY, pursuing a B.A in physics and concentration in math and philosophy. My research lies within the realm of analyzing the atmospheres of exoplanets, brown dwarfs and low mass stars.
After I graduate Hunter in May 2024, I will be attending graduate school at the CUNY Graduate Center to pursue a Ph.D. in Physics with the research group Brown Dwarfs in NYC at the American Museum of Natural History.
Besides research, I am enthusiastic about science communication in both English and Spanish, sharing my love for astronomy with others.
